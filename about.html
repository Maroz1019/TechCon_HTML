History

TechCon began in 2000 as a small meetup of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a major annual event, attracting thousands of participants from around the world. The conference has consistently provided a platform for sharing knowledge, fostering innovation, and building community.

Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to inspire and enable the tech community to build a better future through technology. We strive to create an inclusive environment that promotes learning, collaboration, and growth for everyone, regardless of their background or experience level.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Ada Lovelace is known as the first computer programmer. She has inspired generations of women in tech with her groundbreaking work.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing was a mathematician and logician who made major contributions to computer science, particularly through his work on the concept of the algorithm and the Turing machine.

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper was a pioneer in computer programming, known for her work on the first compiler and for popularizing the idea of machine-independent programming languages.
